Relaxing Yoga Routine

Take a moment to breathe deeply and find your center. Follow these gentle poses to relax your mind and body.

  • 1. Child's Pose

    Begin on your hands and knees, then sit back on your heels, stretching your arms forward. Breathe deeply and hold for 5 breaths.

  • 2. Cat-Cow Stretch

    On all fours, inhale as you arch your back (Cow), and exhale as you round your spine (Cat). Repeat for 5 cycles.

  • 3. Forward Fold

    Stand tall and slowly fold forward, letting your head and arms hang. Breathe and hold for 5 breaths.

  • 4. Legs-Up-The-Wall Pose

    Lie on your back and extend your legs up against a wall. Relax here for 5-10 minutes, focusing on your breath.

  • 5. Savasana (Corpse Pose)

    Lie flat on your back with arms at your sides. Close your eyes and breathe deeply. Stay here for at least 5 minutes.
